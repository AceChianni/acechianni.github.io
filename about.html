My Story

A creative evolution shaped by psychology, art, and intentional rediscovery.

More Than a Designer — A Translator of Feeling

I did not arrive here from a linear path and that is my power. My path winds through psychology, film, holistic wellness, and visual art, all converging into a design practice rooted in emotional intelligence and intentionality.

I create intentional digital experiences for people who feel deeply, think expansively, and move through the world with nuance. My work blends softness with structure. I live for merging intuitive artistry and technical strategy to design interfaces that feel human, embodied, and alive.

I am not interested in design that only looks beautiful. I build experiences that communicate, regulate, and restore.

My story is unconventional. My perspective is intentional. And my work lives at the intersection of soul and system.

A Walk Through Time

March 2020
Pandemic Began

The sudden loss of my first post-grad role as an Executive Assistant became a quiet turning point. Rather than fold, I returned to my creative roots — using this pause as a portal into self-expression, healing, and visual storytelling.

October 2020
Photoshop Certified

Achieved Adobe Photoshop Certification through NOVAC, grounding my creative instincts in technical structure and digital craftsmanship. This marked the beginning of my conscious transition into design as discipline.

2021 – 2022
The Film Chapter

Immersed in the art and costume departments of film production, learning how narrative, emotion, and visual language shape immersive worlds. This phase taught me how environments influence feeling — a lesson that deeply informs my UX approach today.

2023
Freelance Era

Began translating intuition into professional vision through freelance projects — crafting logos, branding, and web concepts for emerging businesses. Here, design evolved from passion into practice.

November 2024
Google UX/UI Design Certificate

Earned Google UX/UI Design Certification, strengthening my foundation in empathy-driven design, usability testing, and accessibility. This solidified my role as a designer who prioritizes human experience over aesthetics alone.

February 2025
Full Stack Developer — CodeX Academy

Completed Full Stack Developer training through CodeX Academy, gaining fluency in front-end and backend workflows. Developed my first big web based project, Inkspresso, a fully functional e-commerce platform bridging design and technical execution.

Present
Creating the Intersection

Currently cultivating Inkspression — an interactive digital journaling platform centered on mindfulness and neuro-aesthetic flow. My focus now lies in creating soulful, sensory digital experiences that honor both form and feeling.
