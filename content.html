Photoshop · Typography

Beauty & The Beast — Minimalist Poster

A minimalist reimagining of the Disney classic, focused on silhouette, negative space, and emotional tone. The poster captures Belle and Beast mid-dance in soft blue hues, anchored by the line: “Who could ever learn to love a beast.”

This study strengthened my approach to hierarchy, symbolism, and typographic pacing while working within a highly constrained visual language.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop Focus: Typography · Composition · Color Theory
Photoshop · Vintage Design

Tokyo, Japan — Retro Travel Postcard

A collage-style postcard that layers found imagery, kanji, and English typography to evoke a nostalgic view of Tokyo. The design combines grain, texture overlays, and selective desaturation to create a worn-in, print-inspired aesthetic.

This project focused on balancing visual noise with clarity and using type, texture, and framing to suggest story and place.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop Focus: Collage · Texture · Typography Hierarchy
Digital Art · Conceptual Design

“Strings Attached” — Trompe L’oeil Self Portrait

A conceptual composite where my back transforms into the body of a guitar, exploring identity, embodiment, and sound as extensions of self. The illusion relies on careful blending, shadow work, and texture alignment.

This piece refined my non-destructive editing workflow and challenged me to integrate human anatomy with inanimate forms in a believable way.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop Focus: Photo Manipulation · Lighting · Layering
Brand Identity · Illustration

Brunch Babes — “Lemme Feed You”

A playful, feminine brand identity concept for Brunch Babes, a one-woman culinary brand specializing in pop-up brunch events and soul food. The visual direction blends softness and confidence through hand-drawn marks, rounded forms, and a comfort-driven palette.

The work includes logo exploration, wordmark refinement, and early applications across social and packaging touchpoints.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop Focus: Identity Exploration · Illustration · Brand Story
